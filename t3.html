Task Description:

Find computational and/or experimental data showing the position of individual dopant atoms in the diamond lattice, either as isolated dopant atoms or preferably in a complex involving several atoms.

Look for pictures of the structure/position of the dopant atoms, together with estimated density/concentration and a table of the electronic and structural properties of the resulting material.

Identify candidate materials to study using the atom probe instrument, to confirm whether these structures existed.
